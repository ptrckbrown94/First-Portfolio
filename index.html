About Me

I have great St. Paul pride, I’m born and raised in the Capital City. I went to college at Iowa State University in Ames, Iowa. I graduated in 2010 with a degree in Elementary Education. I then worked as a financial advisor at multiple financial institutions and the past year I worked in the private banking division at Wells Fargo in Minneapolis.

I live in St. Paul with my wife, our one year old son and our rescue dog. I love to stay active in my free time; walking/hiking with our dog and swimming on the masters team at Macalester College. I enjoy volunteering my time; I’m on the executive board at Park Square Theater in St. Paul and I was a member of the St. Paul Rotary Club for five years.

I look forward to working with a team to build web based applications to help clients achieve their business goals.
